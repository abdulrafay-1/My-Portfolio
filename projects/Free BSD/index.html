Status

The FreeBSD/ppc port is still a Tier 2 platform. That means it is not being fully supported by our secuirity officer, release engineers and toolchain maintainers.

Latest News

  • 03 April, 2009: Support for the PowerPC G5 has been committed to SVN.

  • 03 MArch, 2008: Support for Freescale® PowerQUICC 111 MPC85XX family system-on-chip development boards has been committed to CVS.

  • 27 February, 2008: FreeBSD 7.0 is the first release to offically support the FreeBSD/ppc port.
